The Problem
The problem is bigger
than you think.
For Students

No way to check menus before swiping in

8 dining halls + 10 retail spots, no easy mobile tool

Nutrition info is buried

UConn's site exists but it's clunky and not built for mobile

For Staff & Faculty

Students with allergens feel underserved

Celiac, lactose intolerant, halal - no easy way to filter what's safe

No data on student dietary needs

Hard to know which allergens are most common or where gaps exist

The Solution
One app. Every meal.
For Students

Real-time menus

All 8 dining halls + retail, updated automatically

Health scores (0-99)

Personalized to your health conditions and dietary needs

Food journal

Log meals, track nutrition over time

For Staff & Faculty

"Safe for Me" allergen filtering

One tap to hide items that conflict with student allergens

Allergen analytics

Data on which dietary needs are most common among students

Underserved student signals

Flags when a dining hall has zero safe options for a student
